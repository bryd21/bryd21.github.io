About Modu Tennis

Meet Steven Park

Founder & Head Coach

Steven Park is a passionate tennis professional with over 15 years of coaching experience. His journey in tennis began at a young age, competing in junior tournaments throughout the Northeast before earning a Division I tennis scholarship.

After completing his collegiate career, Steven dedicated himself to coaching, working with players of all ages and skill levels. His coaching philosophy centers on the belief that tennis is not just about winning matches, but about building character, discipline, and a lifelong love for the sport.

Steven founded Modu Tennis with a vision to create a training program that emphasizes technical excellence, mental toughness, and personal growth. He holds USPTA and PTR certifications and has helped numerous students achieve their tennis goals, from making their high school varsity teams to competing at the collegiate level.

Outside of coaching, Steven continues to compete in regional tournaments and stays current with the latest developments in tennis coaching methodology. His commitment to continuous learning ensures that Modu Tennis students receive instruction based on the most effective and modern training techniques.

Our Philosophy

Technical Excellence

We focus on building solid fundamentals and proper technique from day one, ensuring long-term player development.

Mental Strength

Tennis is as much mental as it is physical. We teach players how to stay focused, confident, and resilient on court.

Individualized Approach

Every player is unique. We tailor our coaching to match each student's learning style, goals, and competitive level.

Lifelong Learning

We instill a love for the game that extends beyond competition, encouraging players to enjoy tennis for life.
